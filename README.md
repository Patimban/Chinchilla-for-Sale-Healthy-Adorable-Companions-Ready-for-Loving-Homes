**Basic Care Requirements**

Before bringing home your chinchilla, it’s important to prepare a suitable environment. Chinchillas require:

A large, multi-level wire cage for jumping and climbing
Unlimited high-quality timothy hay
Premium chinchilla pellets
Fresh, clean water daily
Dust baths 2–3 times per week
Chinchillas should never be bathed in water. Instead, they roll in special dust to keep their fur clean and healthy. Maintaining the correct temperature is also crucial. Chinchillas are sensitive to heat and should be kept in a cool room between 60–70°F (15–21°C).

Providing chew toys and wooden ledges will help prevent boredom and promote healthy teeth.
